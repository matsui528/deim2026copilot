Yusuke Matsui

Lecturer, The University of Tokyo

I work on scalable visual search, image retrieval, and approximate nearest neighbor algorithms. My goal is to make billion-scale visual intelligence fast, efficient, and practical.

What I work on

Research Interests

🔍

Image Retrieval

Large-scale content-based image search at billion-scale with compact representations.

⚡

Approximate Nearest Neighbor

Fast ANN search algorithms including product quantization and graph-based indexing.

🧠

Deep Learning for Vision

Feature learning, self-supervised representation learning, and visual embeddings.

🗜️

Vector Quantization

Compression of high-dimensional vectors for memory-efficient similarity search.

🎨

Multimedia Analysis

Sketch-based retrieval, video search, and cross-modal matching.

📦

Open-source Libraries

Building practical tools like rii for real-world vector search.
